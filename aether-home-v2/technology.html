06 LAYER 04 — QMS & METROLOGY DWG AE-OS-05 · REV C
ZEISS CMMFAI · IPQC · SPC<15 µm auto-comp
Quality Management System

Closed-loop metrology, not an inspection station.

Parts are sampled through Zeiss CMM inspection, and measurement data feeds directly back to FacOS and APG — so the system tightens itself as it runs.

First Article Inspection (FAI)
CMM validates initial parts against GD&T; deviations auto-update tool offsets.
In-Process Quality Control (IPQC)
Statistical sampling during runs with real-time SPC charting.
Full traceability
Material lot, machine ID, tool history and inspection results linked to every part.
